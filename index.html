Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the Subscription Cost Calculator?

Simply add each subscription with its name and cost. Choose monthly or yearly billing. The calculator automatically shows your monthly, yearly, and 5-year totals.

How much does the average person spend on subscriptions?

Studies show the average person spends $219 per month on subscriptions - that is over $2,600 per year and $13,000 over 5 years!

Is my data stored?

No. This calculator runs entirely in your browser using localStorage. Your subscription data never leaves your device and is never uploaded to any server.

Can I add subscriptions billed annually?

Yes! Select "Yearly" as your billing cycle and enter your annual cost. The calculator automatically divides by 12 to show your monthly equivalent.

Can I export my subscription data?

Yes, click the "Export CSV" button to download a spreadsheet with all your subscriptions, billing details, and cost totals.
