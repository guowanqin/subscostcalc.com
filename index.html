Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the Subscription Cost Calculator?

Simply add each subscription with its name and monthly cost. The calculator automatically shows your monthly, yearly, and 5-year totals.

How much does the average person spend on subscriptions?

Studies show the average person spends $219 per month on subscriptions — that's over $2,600 per year and $13,000 over 5 years!

Is my data stored?

No. This calculator runs entirely in your browser. Your subscription data stays on your device.

Can I add subscriptions billed annually?

Yes! Just enter the annual cost divided by 12 as your monthly amount.
